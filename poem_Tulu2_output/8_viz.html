My refuge and my solid stone,
In You, my strength is ever grown,
My fortress and my peace, forever known.
In desert lands or weary paths,
My thirst, Your living waters baths,
In hunger's pangs, Your bread sustains,
And in the dark, Your light maintains.
Your goodness leads me to repent,
My sinful ways, before You, bent,
Your grace, a mercy I can't own,
A gift from heaven, freely shown.
In times of trials, I lean on You,
My fears and doubts, before You, few,
